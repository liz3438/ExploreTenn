Welcome to Tennessee!

Tennessee is a state in the southwestern United States. It is bordered by Mississippi to the north, Arkansas
to the west, and Alabama to the south.
It is the 13th-most populous U.S. state, and it is the 16th most populous U.S. state in terms of land area.
Originally, prior to the 1700s, Tennessee was a part of North Carolina. It became a recognized state in 1796.
After the mysterious disappearance of the Mississipian culture, the indigenous inhabitants of Tennessee were made
up of mainly three nations: Cherokee and Creek.
Tennessee is known for Country music, beautiful mountains, and helpful, caring people. It is known as the
"volunteer state" due to the large number of volunteers who fought in the War of 1812.
