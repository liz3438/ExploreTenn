The Cradle of Country Music

Knoxville is a city located in Eastern Tennessee and was incorporated in 1791.
It is known as the "Cradle of Country Music" due to well-known Country Music artists being born there.
It is an urban area and is quickly growing in population. The current population is around 195,889.
It is an urban area. The median income is $52,826 which is $2,311 below the state median income of $55,137.*

*United States Census Bureau. (2024). Knoxville, Tennessee. Retrieved may 16, 2024, from https://data.census.gov/all?q=knoxville%20tennessee
